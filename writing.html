silicon valley, 9.22.2015
and sure, we're close, yet compartmentalized within our own lives.
there may be slight
overlaps,
I'm on the swim team with one person and have the same mathematics teacher as another,
but as far as futures are concerned we
really
aren't
together.
we don't ask recommendations for college counselors.
we don't ask one another
where we're taking tutoring or makeup classes,
what kind of extracurriculars we're doing,
what exceptional events have happened along our way.
we ask each other for advice on life decisions,
but
we'd rarely take up that advice.
we'd never discuss our lives as soon as futures are involved,
and why?
are we
scared of the future,
scared of the thought that it and time will unravel us, pulling us to opposite sides of the globe?
scared of the thought that someone else will be more successful than ourselves, or the opposite, that we'll hurt someone close in being showy of our own successes?
